Lexcaliber is an ongoing project to develop novel recommendation and analysis algorithms for legal research.

Our current research focuses include an empirical analysis of circuit splits in the federal judiciary, context-assisted network representation, and a prototype web app to demo the project’s capabilities for legal researchers.
